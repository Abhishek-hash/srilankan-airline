Criteria For Online Check-in with SriLankan Airlines:

  • Online web check-in for Sri Lankan Airlines flights is available from 24 hours to 2 hours before departure.
  • You can also check in from Sri Lankan Airlines counters at airports 1 hour before your flight departs.
  • When you have checked in online with hand luggage, you can go straight to the departure gate with your e-boarding pass 45 minutes before the flight departs.
  • When you have checked in online with checked baggage, you should be at the airport check-in counter 90 minutes before the flight departs with our e-boarding pass.
  • If your departure is from any part of India, you must carry your boarding pass from the airport check-in counter of Sri Lankan Airlines if you have your boarding pass printed.
